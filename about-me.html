What can I tell you about us?

Photoflock is almost certainly the best online photo management and sharing application in the world.PhotoFlock will be a digital portfolio website for photographers all over the world, from naïve to professionals. With its intuitive design, customized for each photographer, the website will be an utter joy for the visitors, providing them with an enhanced experience of the photographer’s work. As a photographer, their portfolio website is the business card for them in the digital world. Keeping this very idea in mind, an online presence is necessary for showing their work to the world and reaching out to potential clients. The photographer’s online presence should not be limited to just our website. Social media is a great way to promote their work and their online portfolio. It also makes it easier for people to contact the photographer. So, we would also be adding links from the photographer’s social media sites to not only promote their work but also to show a different side of the person as a photographer. A photographer’s website would consist of their previous work, partnership information, devices they use, contact information etc. If a company or anyone wants to hire a photographer, this website would act as a photographer’s resume which may help them land in their dream job.
